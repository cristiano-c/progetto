Welcome all!

In this website you can create some recipes (called IFTTT Polito Recipes ). A recipe is a chain of two elements; the former is the trigger and the latter is the action. When tomorrow rains (trigger) you could be get advised by an email (action). This is a simple example, go further to create more complex recipes and have fun!
